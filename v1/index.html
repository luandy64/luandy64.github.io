About Me

Hello there! I am a recent graduate with a degree in Applied Mathematics, with an emphasis on Computational Science, and minors in Computer Science and the Natural Sciences. Over the last four years, I have honed my analytical skills and applied them to projects ranging from a static code analyzer to a distributed multi-dimensional integration tool to a model of a primate retinal neuron. Outside of class, my coding projects vary as well. Through an internship with Revature, I learned to deploy a Java web app to facilitate the buying and selling of ebooks. In my spare time, I've been working through the freeCodeCamp curriculum and learning web development.

I've had a lot of leadership experience over the years. At my university, I was the Math Curriculum Chair for CollegeBound, a service group tackling education inequality at a local Dallas high school by providing free SAT tutoring. Beyond that, I used to teach martial arts- specifically Taekwondo, Brazillian Jiu-jitsu, and Muay Thai. That was always such a rewarding experience because I got to teach and learn from and grow with people of all ages and backgrounds.
